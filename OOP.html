Object Oriented Programing

Objects

Think of some objects in real life. Well all objects have two things: qualities and actions.

In terms of programming, an object would be a class, the attributes of an object is the qualities of it and the methods of an object is the possible actions of an object.

An example, cars can be from different brands, makes and models. They also have different fuel efficencies, cargo space, widths, prices, and engines.
